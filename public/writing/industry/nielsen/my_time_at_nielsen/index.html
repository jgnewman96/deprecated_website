What I Did at Nielsen

My first job after completing undergrad was as a Data Scientist at Nielsen. Nielsen is a market research company that provides insight about what people buy and what media content they consume. I was a part of Nielsen’s early career program focused on fast tracking individuals to leadership roles. The program consists of four six months rotations for a total of two years.
